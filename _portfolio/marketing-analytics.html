Introduction

This project simulates a retail business that is facing a drop in customer engagement and conversion rates, despite the launching of several new marketing campaigns.

These are the main key points the company wishes to solve:

  • Reduced Customer Feedback: The number of feedback entries and engagement with the site has declined
  • High Marketing Expenses: Significant investments in marketing campaigns don’t have the expected returns
  • Need for Customer Feedback Analysis: Understanding customer opinions about the products and services is crucial for improving engagement and conversions

Data Source

The original dataset and business problem were created by Ali Ahmad, which inspired this project.

The database contains the following tables:

  • Customer Journey Table: Tracks customer movements through the website to analyze the conversion funnel
  • Engagement Data Table: Measures engagement with different types of content
  • Customer Reviews Table: Analyzes customer feedback to identify common themes
  • Customer Table: Provides additional information about customers
  • Geography Table: Provides additional geographic information about customers
  • Product Table: Provides additional information about products

Tools

  • SQL: Used to clean and transform the data loaded in SQL Server
  • Python: Used to calculate sentiment scores based on customer feedback and to create sentiment categories
  • Power BI: Used to model and create the data visualizations

Findings

Note: Since this is fictional data, the dataset includes information for the entire year of 2025. However, since the year is not complete, I will only interpret the data related to 2023 and 2024.

Conversion Rates

  • The average conversion rate by year decreased by 3.5% from 2023 to 2024, with the lowest conversion rate occurring in May 2024 (4.5%), a drop of 4.4% compared to the previous year.
  • The percentage of customers who made a purchase after clicking a page on the website also decreased in 2024 (16%), a decline of 5.6% compared to 2023 (21.6%).
  • The products that yielded the highest conversion rates in 2023 were Hockey Stick (21.6%), Yoga Mat (18.8%), and Ice Skates (18.4%). In 2024, the products with the highest conversion rates were Ski Boots (20.7%), Kayak (17.9%), and Surfboard (13.9%).
  • In both years, the month with the highest conversion rate was January.
  • In 2023, the lowest conversion rates were observed in April (7.8%) and September (4.2%). In 2024, the lowest conversion rates occurred in May (4.5%) and October (5.1%).

Social Media

  • The average number of views and clicks per year dropped by almost half from 2023 to 2024.
  • The yearly average number of likes dropped by almost 78% from 2023 to 2024.
  • The decline in views was also observed across all content types (Blog, Social Media, and Video).
  • In 2023, the two most viewed products were Swim Goggles (most viewed through videos) and Climbing Rope (most viewed through blog posts).
  • In 2024, the products with the highest views were Ice Skates (most viewed on blogs) and Cycling Helmet (most viewed through videos).

Customer Reviews

  • The company’s goal is to improve the average rating to 4. Both 2023 (3.73) and 2024 (3.67) were below this target.
  • Despite this, the highest number of reviews in both years had a rating of 4.
  • Reviews were generally classified as Positive in the sentiment analysis.
  • A common theme in negative reviews was delayed delivery and product quality issues.
  • Some mixed or negative reviews also mentioned unclear product instructions.
